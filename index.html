Biographical Sketch

I'm a Ph.D student in the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ICT, CAS). I received a B.S. degree from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2008 and Master degree from Inner Mongolian University in 2013 as a joint training student with ICT, both in Computer Science. I worked for Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ISCAS) as a senior engineer from July, 2008 to September, 2009. From October 20th, 2015, I will join the Department of Computer Science at University of Chicago as a visiting student, under the supervison of Professor Fred Chong. The visiting is funded by a national awared scholarship from China Scholarship Council(CSC) and will last for one year.
